Who we are

KUNENE KHRONICLES, IS A CREATIVE INVESTMENT COMPANY DEDICATED TO EMPOWERING SMALL BUSINESSES, ENTREPRENEURS AND ARTISTS THROUGH FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE, MENTORSHIP, AND BRAND DEVELOPMENT

OUR VALUES

Diversity and Inclusion: We believe in celebrating the rich tapestry of human culture and creativity by embracing diversity and providing a platform for artists from all backgrounds.
Artistic Integrity: We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of artistic quality and promoting authentic expressions that resonate with audiences on a profound level.
Cultural Preservation: \We recognize the importance of preserving cultural heritage and providing a space for stories that might otherwise be forgotten, ensuring that traditions and narratives are passed down to future generations.
Community Engagement: We aim to build a global community of art enthusiasts, filmmakers, musicians, and artists who share a passion for creativity and the exploration of human expression

OUR MISSION 

Our mission at Kunene Khronicles is to celebrate and preserve cultural heritage, foster artistic innovation, and provide a space for artists and creators to share their stories, talents, and passions with a global audience. We are dedicated to curating a collection of captivating short films, soul stirring music, and exceptional artworks that resonate with people from all walks of life. 

OUR VIS ION 

Kunene Khronicles envisions becoming a hub for artistic collaboration and innovation, fostering partnerships with creators, institutions, and organizations worldwide. Through continuous expansion of our digital museum, music collection, and online art store, we strive to inspire and enrich the lives of individuals while contributing to the global cultural landscape. Kunene Khronicles invites you to embark on a journey of discovery, creativity, and connection. Experience the magic of short films, the power of music, and the allure of exceptional art—all in one digital destination. Join us in celebrating the boundless potential of human expression at Kunene Khronicles.

MEET AYANDA KUNENE, THE FOUNDER 

AYANDA KUNENE IS A DEDICATED FINAL-YEAR SOCIAL WORK STUDENT AT THE UNIVERSITY OF THE WITWATERSRAND. HER PASSION LIES IN EMPOWERING INDIVIDUALS AND COMMUNITIES TO ACHIEVE THEIR FULLEST POTENTIAL THROUGH HER PROFESSIONAL PRACTICE. THIS PASSION IS DEEPLY ROOTED IN HER EXPERIENCES, WHERE SHE HAS SEEN FIRSTHAND THE TRANSFORMATIVE POWER OF SOCIAL WORK IN IMPROVING LIVES AND FOSTERING RESILIENCE. A PIVOTAL MOMENT IN AYANDA'S JOURNEY WAS LOSING HER BURSARY, WHICH BECAME A SIGNIFICANT TURNING POINT. INSTEAD OF BEING DETERRED, SHE USED THIS CHALLENGE AS MOTIVATION TO PURSUE HER ENTREPRENEURIAL VISION, LEADING TO THE CREATION OF KUNENE KHRONICLES. THIS INITIATIVE IS NOT JUST A BUSINESS VENTURE BUT A MANIFESTATION OF AYANDA’S DETERMINATION TO MAKE A TANGIBLE IMPACT IN HER COMMUNITY. THROUGH KUNENE KHRONICLES, SHE SEEKS TO ADDRESS UNEMPLOYMENT BY CREATING OPPORTUNITIES FOR OTHERS WHILE CONTRIBUTING TO LOCAL ECONOMIC GROWTH. AYANDA'S APPROACH IS GROUNDED IN INTEGRITY, A CORE VALUE THAT GUIDES HER DECISIONS AND INTERACTIONS. SHE ENVISIONS KUNENE KHRONICLES AS A PLATFORM THAT NOT ONLY SUPPORTS SMALL BUSINESSES BUT ALSO SERVES AS A BEACON OF EMPOWERMENT AND INNOVATION. BY COMBINING HER PROFESSIONAL EXPERTISE IN SOCIAL WORK WITH HER ENTREPRENEURIAL ASPIRATIONS, AYANDA IS COMMITTED TO BUILDING A LEGACY THAT UPLIFTS INDIVIDUALS AND STRENGTHENS COMMUNITIES.
