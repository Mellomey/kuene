ABOUT THE COMPANY 

Kunene Khronicles is a captivating digital museum that offers an immersive experience through a combination of short films, music, and an online store showcasing unique artworks. As a hub for artistic expression and cultural exploration, Kunene Khronicles aims to enrich the lives of individuals by connecting them with diverse forms of creativity and providing a platform for artists to exhibit and sell their art.
